Is This Tech Dead?

An Empirical Analysis of Technological Relevance
7/4/2026

Abstract

This document presents an ongoing, highly scientific, and definitely unbiased analysis of various technologies to determine their level of relevance, or 'Deaditude'. We examine numerous extremely objective indicators to provide a definitive score. Below is the current registry of technologies and their respective existential statuses.

Published by: The Open Source Community
Journal of Deprecated Technologies

1. Introduction

In the rapidly evolving landscape of software engineering, tools and frameworks frequently fall out of favor, leaving developers stranded with deprecated APIs and existential dread. This research aims to quantify this phenomenon so you can finally win arguments on Hacker News. For details on our flawless methodology, please refer to theMethodology Section.
